About Finest

From personal ambition to community impact

Finest Mwamba wrote Student Life Zambia to give practical encouragement to students and young people. Through his book, talks and future publications, he wants to inspire readers to make better decisions, develop a positive mindset and pursue their full potential.

He is now building his platform as an author, public speaker and youth mentor, while preparing his second book focused on positivity and mindset.
